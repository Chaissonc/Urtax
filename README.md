# urtax. 💸
### Your personal money system.
 
**urtax** is a lightweight, customizable budgeting tool that lets you allocate your income into custom spending buckets — however you want, however many you need. No accounts. No complexity. Just your money, your way.
 
🔗 **Live App:** [chaissonc.github.io/Urtax](https://chaissonc.github.io/Urtax/)
 
---
 
## What It Does
 
Enter your income or budget, create your own spending categories, and assign percentages to each one. urtax instantly calculates the dollar amount for every bucket so you always know exactly where your money goes.
 
Think of it like a digital envelope system, but fully customizable and built for how you **actually** live.
 
---
 
## Features
 
- 💰 **Custom Money Buckets** — Create, name, and assign any category you want (rent, groceries, savings, fun money, anything)
- ➕ **Add & Remove Categories** — Full control over your budget layout
- 📊 **Live Percentage Tracking** — See your total and remaining percentage update in real time
- 📱 **Mobile Friendly** — Numeric decimal keypad on mobile for fast input
- 🕓 **Site History** — Revisit past budget sessions
- 🟩 **Clean UI** — Smooth scrolling with a polished green theme, cards that stack neatly on any screen size
- 📄 **Blank Template** — Start completely fresh with a clean slate
- ❌ **No** Silly Signups.
